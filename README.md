\# Retail Growth Analytics and Customer Insights



\## Objective



Analyze retail transaction data to identify customer purchasing patterns, revenue drivers, and business growth opportunities.



\## Tools Used



\* Python

\* Pandas

\* Matplotlib



\## Key Insights



\* December recorded the highest sales performance.

\* San Francisco generated the highest revenue.

\* AAA Batteries were the most frequently purchased product.

\* Customer activity peaked during late morning and evening hours.



\## Business Recommendations



\* Increase marketing efforts during high-demand periods.

\* Optimize inventory planning before seasonal demand spikes.

\* Bundle frequently purchased products to increase average order value.

\* Focus campaigns on high-performing regions.
